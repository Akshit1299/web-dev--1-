The Conjuring House is the real-life 1736 farmhouse in Harrisville, Rhode Island, where the Perron family reported experiencing intense supernatural phenomena in the 1970s, leading to their involvement with paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren and inspiring the film The Conjuring. The property continues to attract interest from ghost hunters and tourists for its historical significance and ongoing alleged paranormal activity.

K.R. Mangalam University (KRMU) is a private university in Gurugram, India, established in 2013 under the Haryana Private University Act. It offers interdisciplinary education with a focus on research and innovation to foster socially responsible, lifelong learners for nation-building. Recognized by the UGC and various professional bodies, KRMU provides modern infrastructure, a Wi-Fi-enabled campus, and a comprehensive learning environment for its students
